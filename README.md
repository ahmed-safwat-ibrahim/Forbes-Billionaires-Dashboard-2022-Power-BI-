# Forbes Billionaires 2022 Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview
This project involves the development of an interactive **Power BI dashboard** to analyze and visualize the wealth, demographics, and industry distribution of the world’s top billionaires in 2022. The dashboard provides insights into the concentration of wealth by age, country, industry, and self-made status, supporting strategic decision-making for financial analysts, investors, and market researchers.

The dashboard translates complex datasets into **actionable intelligence** for understanding global wealth distribution trends, identifying high-growth sectors, and assessing opportunities for investment or market positioning.
---
## Key Features

* **Global Wealth Overview:**
  * Total Billionaires: 2,670
  * Total Net Worth: $1.27 Trillion
  * Average Net Worth: $476.24M
  * Billionaires’ Net Worth Distribution by Age

* **Demographics & Gender Analysis:**
  * Male vs Female composition (87.7% Male, 11.7% Female)
  * Self-Made vs Inherited wealth distribution (29.1% Self-Made)

* **Geographic Insights:**
  * Top countries by billionaire count and total wealth (e.g., USA: 748 billionaires, $475B total worth)
  * Heatmap visualization of billionaire density by country

* **Industry Insights:**
  * Top 5 industries by net worth: Technology, Finance & Investments, Fashion & Retail, Manufacturing, Diversified
  * Interactive donut chart showing sectoral composition of billionaires

* **Ranking & Detailed Profiles:**
  * Table view listing individual billionaires with Rank, Net Worth, Industry, Country, and Birthdate
  * Filterable and sortable for detailed comparisons

---
## Business & Market Impact

* **Investor Insights:** The analysis helps identify industries with the highest concentration of wealth, signaling sectors with strong market potential.
* **Geopolitical & Market Strategy:** Country-level wealth data highlights strategic markets for investment, market expansion, or competitive intelligence.
---
## Data Model & Methodology
* **Star Schema Design:** Fact table (`Billionaire Data`) at the center with connected dimensions for `Country`, `Industry`, `Gender`, and `Age`.
* **Advanced Visualizations:**

  * Line charts for age vs total net worth
  * Heatmaps for geographic distribution
  * Donut charts for industry and gender distribution
  * Interactive tables for drill-down analysis

* **Filters & Interactivity:**

  * Country, Industry, and Age filters to explore trends and identify high-impact insights.
  * Self-Made filter highlights entrepreneurs versus inherited wealth holders.
---
## Tools & Skills Applied

* **Power BI:** Dashboard development, DAX calculations, interactive visuals, slicers, and drill-down functionality
* **Data Analysis:** Wealth distribution, demographic segmentation, and market trend identification
